The Atlas Scientific PWR-SNS has been specifically designed for devices where power monitoring and control capabilities need to be taken seriously.


Hardware features

• Monitor and control up to 100 watts

continuously with a microcontroller

• Voltage monitoring

• Current monitoring

• Wattage monitoring

• Joules (watt seconds) monitoring

• On board temperature monitoring

• Courtesy 3.3V @1 amp power out pin.

• Temperature protected operation with thermal

cutoff at 75˚C (167˚F)

• Simple calibration

• Single reading, or continuous reading mode

• Simple asynchronous serial connectivity with

8 different baud rates

• Automatic baud rate detection

• Debugging LED's

• Wide operational and control voltage

(4.3VDC to 20VDC)

• Low power consumption

4.4 mA at 4.3V in active mode*

4.2 mA at 4.3V in quiescent mode*

*LED's off
