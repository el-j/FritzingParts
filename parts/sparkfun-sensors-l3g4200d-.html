L3G4200D Three-axis, digital output gyroscope

The L3G4200D is a low-power three-axis angular rate sensor, has a full scale of ±250/±500/±2000 dps and is capable of measuring rates with a user-selectable bandwidth.

It includes a sensing element and an IC interface capable of providing the measured angular rate to the external world through a digital interface (I2C/SPI).
Package: 4x4x1.1 LGA-16 (LGA16-4x4.PAC)
