Description:

Need to get all of your Atlas Scientific equipment calibrated and running smoothly in the shortest amount of time?


This Debugger is specially designed to have its pin outs match up directly with all of the Atlas Scientific Circuits. Just plug it into your bread board and your ready to go.


• Rapid calibration and debugging

• 2 different output voltages (3.3 and 5.0)

• Header pins directly line up to any Atlas Scientific Circuits

• This is the exact device used here at Atlas Scientific to test all of

our sensors

• Works as a simple USB to serial converter when not being used to calibrate/debug
