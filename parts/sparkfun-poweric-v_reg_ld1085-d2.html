LD1085 3A low-drop positive voltage regulator, adjustable

The LD1085xx is a low drop voltage regulator able to provide up to 3 A of output current. Dropout is guaranteed at a maximum of 1.2 V at the maximum output current, decreasing at lower loads. The LD1085xx is pin to pin compatible with the older 3-terminal adjustable regulators, but has better performances in term of drop and output tolerance.
